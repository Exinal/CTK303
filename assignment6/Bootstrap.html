Our Teams

Elite Doll Squads

These are some of the main allies who are very prominent in the story

AR Team (Anti Rain)

The first squad you start off with and the team whos story impacts you the most. This squad of dolls were all specially made and as such, have more volatile emotions than other dolls and were unable to make backups of. This made the threat of permanently losing these dolls very powerful, is a major drawback of this otherwise powerful squad.

To Wiki

Squad 404

An elite squad of four dolls who are specialized in electronic warfare and are capable of acting illegally witout human orders. Because of this, the squad was used as a covert squad who would mostly act independtly and provide support to the main squad via electronic disruption and eliminating lower value targets who would exhaust the main squad

To Wiki

Task Force DEFY

An elite squad of four dolls who work under the agent Angelia. They are special purpose built for her and greatly help us in our missions. Each member has their own unique properties, such as one having immense physical strength at and abnormal level for most dolls.

To Wiki

Enemies

The enemies who oppose our main character and we must regularly fight against to achieve our goals

Sangvis Ferri

A Romanian arms manufacturer, later turned violent non-state actor, and is one of the first antagonistic faction for the game. After eventually beating down this faction, we manage to gain control of some of their dolls and use them for our own sake later down the road.

To Wiki

Paradeus

A violent non-state actor based in Germany, described as a terrorist group and a cult. They show up more frequently and eventually become the main enemy later on after the Sangvis Ferri are taken care of. Havin done lots of experimentation all for the sole purpose of trying to revive someone, with all these experiments leading to many side effects which greatly impacted the world.

To Wiki

KCCO

A Russian, later Neo-Soviet strategic level special forces command and a subbranch of the Neo-Soviet Army,which later turned into a rogue military force. They are constantly fighting against you throughout the story, with their different affiliations changing but always disruptive to our goals
